    SYNOPSIS






    In Sto. Nino Mactan Montessori School, "The Missing Note of My Heart" weaves a tale of Mariel and Dale, whose lives intersect unexpectedly. Mariel is a dedicated student known for her academic prowess and singing talent. In contrast, Dale is a troubled delinquent entangled in the world of high school gangs, hiding a deep passion for music. Their paths cross when Dale, intrigued by Mariel's singing, joins her in the glee club. Through late-night music sessions, Mariel teaches Dale guitar, sparking a transformation in him as he distances himself from his turbulent past. However, Dale's former gang seeks revenge, leading to a tragic accident that claims his life. Despite the heartbreak, their story reflects the profound impact of music and love in navigating life's challenges.
